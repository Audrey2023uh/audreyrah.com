About Me

I am a dedicated Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Houston, focused on advancing research in emerging technologies and innovative solutions for real-world challenges.

My research interests span multiple domains within electrical and computer engineering, with a particular emphasis on practical applications and interdisciplinary approaches to problem-solving.

Research Areas

My research encompasses various critical areas including artificial intelligence and machine learning applications, data science architectures, cybersecurity governance, wireless networking systems, FPGA design, virtual reality analytics, and software automation.
